Our Story

A Living
Heritage Born
From the Forest

Tucked into the rolling hills of Bucovina, where ancient firs touch the clouds and meadows glow gold at dusk, Hanul Bucovinei has welcomed wanderers and dreamers for over a century.

Each room carries the soul of the land — carved oak, hand-woven textiles, the scent of pine — woven together with a quiet luxury that never overwhelms, only envelops.
